SWOT Analysis
Internal Strengths Weaknesses
  • What are we doing good on our website that we should keep on the redesign?
    • Hero image isn’t bad - I like the “ABC” art. Nav is good. “Meet our Baristas” is good engaging content
  • Is there anything we could potentially highlight on the website that will display the traits that make our organization enjoyable to work for or be a customer of?
    • Highlight the baristas better - carousel?
    • Show more images of coffee and donuts - large product landing page?
    • Maybe another carousel of recent customer reviews.
  • What are we best known for? What makes us different (in a positive light) from our competitors? (i.e.: customer service, quality products, etc.)
    • Coffee & donuts. We’re different because of donuts. We’re personable, friendly. Drive-thru available. More of a personal touch due to being a small business.
  • Are there any aspects in the usability of our website that would affect our target audience?
    • Navigation needs to be cleaned up/organized better.
    • Mobile site needs work.
    • Needs alt text, badly, for images. “Title” tag for links.
    • Contrast in menu colors (or colors in general)
    • Need a menu for boba tea
  • What are some of the complaints that we receive from our website that we could fix?
    • Menu doesn’t have pictures of any of the drinks, or descriptions for the items. No prices for items either.
  • Is there something that is currently non-existent that should be put in place?
    • Customer loyalty/newsletter program. Carousel of recent social media posts. Showcase reviews/testimonials?
    • “Order online” or “order here” or link to doordash/UberEats - make it static across all pages (nav bar?)
External Opportunities Threats
  • Is there a change in the economy, market, politics that the company can benefit from and should think about as we are redesigning our website?
    • Doordash/delivery options. Social media marketing is mandatory, not optional. Coldbrew should be an option. Offer limited boutique coffee roasts (beans or pre-ground) - sell online as well. Seasonal drinks.
  • Is there a change in the population which could potentially change the target audience?
    • Target younger audience and their preference for boba/cold coffee
    • Delivery = target people who are staying at home (contactless delivery)
    • Men are becoming more and more comfortable buying coffee other than espresso/black coffee - make something to target that audience.
  • Is there new technology that is available to help propel the goals for the company that may not have existed in the past?
    • TikTok - younger employees - generate customer engagement.
    • Release an app for customers - ordering, paying, loyalty program - rewards system. Stamp cards?
    • Alliances: 1) target teachers 2) students - college, mainly 3) nurses/healthcare workers. Free coffee/donuts for essential workers. Coupons. “Like this/share this” for discounts/marketing.
  • The organization may benefit by having contingency plans to address them should they occur such as notifications, advertisements for specific products, communication with specific departments, alliances with other businesses, etc.
    • Biggest threat: government shuts down restaurants again.
    • Other coffee shops. Bad PR. Location - need to move?
  • Classify them by their “seriousness” and “probability of occurrence”
    1. Most serious and most likely: government shut down.
    2. Next most serious: bad PR. Not that likely, but possible.
    3. Location: much less likely, not that serious due to delivery option, drive-thru.
